Reminders & golden rules
Tips & settings
🔑 Golden rules
⏰
Eat on time, every time. Set alarms: 9:30 AM breakfast · 1 PM snack · 3 PM lunch · 6 PM snack · 9:30 PM dinner.
🌙
Stop eating by 10 PM. Late-night eating = belly fat. This single rule alone loses you 3–4 kg.
🍽️
Chew slowly — 20 minutes per meal. Brain registers fullness after 20 min. Eating fast = always overeating.
🛒
Sunday meal prep. Cook dal, soak chana, chop veggies for the week. ₹150–200 covers 3–4 days of meals.
⚖️
Weigh in every Monday only. Same time, after washroom. Daily weighing causes anxiety — track weekly trends.
😴
Sleep 7–8 hours. Sleep deprivation causes hunger hormones to spike. Bad sleep = weight gain even on a good diet.
